Start crafting your BentoBox!

BentoBox is a plugin that does nothing on its own, but once you add Gamemodes and Addons to it, it becomes an incredible playground for your players, with countless possible combinations that will allow you to highly customize your server.

Crafting your own BentoBox is the first step to join a world of never-ending possibilities of games and fun.
